Welcome to Jiateng Home Page !

Hi there ! Welcome to my personal homepage!

This is Jiateng Liu (刘嘉腾). I'm currently a second year MS student at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) instructed by Prof. Heng Ji. Before that, I got my bachelor degree in computer science from Zhejiang University.

I am deeply interested in Natural Language Processing and its applications, with a particular focus on the alignment between language and other modalities such as video, audio, and multi-sensory inputs. I believe that language will not only play a central role in the high-level control processes executed by humans, but also as a supervisory signal for extracting features from other modalities to achieve more effective modality fusion. Previously, I have conducted research on knowledgeable Large Language Models (LLMs), ensuring that current LLMs are safe enough for storing and modifying knowledge to serve as a robust backbone for related applications. I am also deeply concerned about the social impact of language technologies; I have worked on detecting and explaining propaganda from social media platforms to aviod our people being misled by disinformation.

I'm looking for a Ph.D position in 2025 Fall!
